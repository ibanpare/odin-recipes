Hummus

Description

A creamy and smooth Middle Eastern dip made from chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. Drizzled with olive oil and sprinkled with paprika, itâ€™s served with fresh pita bread and vegetable sticks for a delightful appetizer.

Ingredients

For 2 people

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and salt in a food processor.
  2. Add water gradually, while blending, to achieve a smooth consistency.
  3. Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Serve with pita bread or vegetable sticks.
