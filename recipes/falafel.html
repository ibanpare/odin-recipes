Falafel with Tahini Sauce

Description

Crispy, golden-brown falafel balls paired with fresh vegetables and a creamy Tahini dipping sauce. This Middle Eastern classic offers a delicious combination of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

For 2 people

For the falafel

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

For the Tahini Sauce

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 cup water (adjust for consistency)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Make the Falafel:
    • Blend chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cilantro, cumin, coriander, baking soda, salt, and pepper in a food processor until a coarse paste forms.
    • Chill the mixture for 30 minutes.
    • Form small balls or patties.
    • Heat oil in a deep pan and fry falafel until golden brown.
  2. Prepare the Tahini Sauce:
    • Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, garlic, water, and salt until smooth.
  3. Serve:
    • Arrange falafel on a plate with fresh vegetables, pita bread, and the Tahini Sauce for dipping.
