Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti lightly coated in olive oil, saut√©ed garlic slices, and a hint of chili flakes. Garnished with fresh parsley, this simple yet flavorful dish is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

For 1 person

  • 125g spaghetti
  • 2 garlic gloves, crushed
  • olive oil (to taste)
  • salt (to taste)
  • chili flakes (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In the meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic slices, and cook until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Toss the drained spaghetti in the pan, adding a splash of reserved pasta water to coat.
  5. Garnish with Parmesan cheese to taste and serve hot.
